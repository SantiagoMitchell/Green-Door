B Corp Certification

B Corp is a certification that recognizes companies that meet rigorous standards for social and environmental performance, accountability, and transparency. To become a B Corp certified company, a business must undergo a comprehensive assessment that evaluates its impact on workers, customers, suppliers, and the environment. This certification is not specific to the food industry, but many food companies have obtained it to demonstrate their commitment to sustainable practices.

One of the benefits of obtaining a B Corp certification is that it can help food companies differentiate themselves in an increasingly competitive market. Consumers are becoming more conscious of the impact their food choices have on the environment and are seeking out products from companies that share their values. By obtaining a B Corp certification, companies can demonstrate their commitment to sustainability and ethical practices and attract consumers who are looking for environmentally friendly options.

earth.com Certification

Earth.com is another certification that recognizes companies for their commitment to sustainability. This certification is specific to the food industry and is awarded to companies that demonstrate a commitment to ethical and sustainable practices in their supply chain. Companies that obtain an earth.com certification must meet a set of criteria related to environmental impact, animal welfare, and fair labor practices.

One of the benefits of obtaining an earth.com certification is that it can help to drive positive change in the food industry. By setting high standards for social and environmental performance, this certification encourages companies to adopt more sustainable practices and hold them accountable for their impact on the environment and society. Additionally, it provides consumers with more information about the products they are purchasing, which can help them make more informed decisions and support companies that share their values.

Sustainable Jungle Certification

Sustainable Jungle also evaluates companies based on their commitment to transparency and social responsibility.
